Visual Art

Is anyone a self taught artist? It is probably easier to say these days, but in reality, most of us have learned something from someone. Practice is what brings anything to fruition, so in that sense, even those who study under others are self taught. I am a bit unsure of who has taught me what. I had some art classes when I was younger, I've watched some Bob Ross, and I had an art class in middle school. Beyond that, though, I have had the vast resources of the internet to help me learn some skills here and there. Yet, I think as far as the art world would be concerned, I'm a self-taught amateur artist.
