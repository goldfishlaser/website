Clifford Elmer Allen

Mr. Clifford E. Allen, age 68 of Conyers, died Sunday, June 22, 2014.

He is survived by his daughter, Melanie Allen, Sandy Springs; sisters and brothers-in-law, Carolyn and Larry Caldwell, Greer, SC, Frances and Jack Brown, Las Vegas, NV, LouDean Mays, Newport News, VA, Nancy Allen, Marietta; brothers and sisters-in-law, Douglas and Lee Allen, Riverdale, Ira and Linda Allen, Lawrenceville, Ron and Maria Allen, Sandy Springs; wife, Wanda Allen, Plainfield, IN; and numerous nieces and nephews. Mr. Allen was a hard worker with a passion for gardening and tinkering and loved his daughter, Melanie.

A Memorial Service will be held Thursday, June 26, 2014 at 4 p.m. at Scot Ward Funeral Services; the family will receive friends from 3 until 4 p.m. prior to the service at the funeral home. Condolences may be submitted on-line at www.scotward.com. Scot Ward Funeral Services, 699 Amercian Legion Road, Conyers, GA 30012, 770-483-7216.
