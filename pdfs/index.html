About this portfolio

The fractal is the theme of my portfolio because it is uniquely suited to reflect the nature of evolving identity. As we iterate through the scenarios of our lives, we have a tremendous capacity to grow, change, and reorganize, while still preserving the essential pattern-nature that makes us recognizable as an individuals.

Fractals exist frequently in natural forms, in the branches of trees, the shells of marine life, in lightning, even in the computations of our brain. The simplest kinds of fractals are created using self-similarity, a smaller size version of an image is attached to a parent, and this is repeated indefinitely, so that as you zoom in, you see the pattern repeated into infinity.
