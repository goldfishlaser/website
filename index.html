About this portfolio

I created this portfolio to meet the requirements of my degree program's capstone class. I graduated with a B.S. Technical Communication in December 2012. You will find artifacts I created during school and work in categories reflecting the skillset they represent.

I made the fractal the theme of my portfolio to reflect the nature of complex evolving identity. Fractals exist frequently in natural forms, the branches of trees, the shells of marine life, lightning, even the computations of our brain. As we iterate through the scenarios of our lives, fractals exist there as well, in that we have a tremendous capacity to grow, change, and reorganize, while still preserving the essential pattern-nature recognizable as ourselves.

I created this web site's background image using the fractal explorer software program Xaos.
