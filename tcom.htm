Technical and professional writing is the practice of presenting information to specialized audiences. The language and rhetoric widely varies between audiences in the coorporate, government, educational, and technical fields, but my coursework has equipped me with writing style versatility. These artifacts show that I can write proposals, memos and other internal documents, research reports, essays, and work together with others in group projects.

These artifacts highlight my profiencies at task oriented instruction and information mapping, as well as my ability to use multiple types of documentation authoring software such as MadCap Flare, Framemaker, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, and Microsoft Office.
