Bill of Materials

An extruder is made of both plastic and non-plastic parts. The plastic parts can be printed off of a RepRap or RepStrap printer, or bought online at Ebay, Ultimachine, or a host of other web sites.

 

See "Downloading parts"

Printed Parts:

  • Large gear
  • Small gear
  • Motor plate
  • Fixed block
  • Base plate


Non-plastic parts:

  • Hobbed bolt
  • Washers
  • Nut
  • Screws
  • Skateboard bearings
  • Hot end
  • Stepper motor
  • Heater
  • Thermistor
