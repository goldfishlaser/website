Choosing an extruder

Extruders differ based on what kind of filament it will print. Filaments vary both in size and in material. Most extruders print ABS or PLA and 1.75 mm or 3 mm diameter plastic, but some extruders are made to print from recycled plastic, metal, and even chocolate.

 

The beginner should likely go with Wade's Geared or Greg's Accessible. These two designs are popular and the community will be especially able to help with any problems that arise. The great thing about RepRap is that once you've printed out one extruder, you can use that extruder to print out different ones, and switch them out based on what you are printing.
