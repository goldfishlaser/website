Hobbing the bolt

Extruders based on Wade's have a hobbed bolt to pull the filament through the chamber. You can buy a bolt pre-hobbed online, but some prefer to do it themselves to save money or to ensure the groove is properly placed.

source: http://reprap.org/wiki/Wade%27s_Geared_Extruder

Hobbing a bolt takes three simple steps.

  1. Insert the bolt into the drill as though it was a drill bit.
  2. Apply a file at the desired location of the groove.
  3. Allow the bolt to rotate on the file until the hob is deep enough.
