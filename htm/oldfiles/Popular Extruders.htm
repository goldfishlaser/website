Introduction to extruders

What is an extruder?

The extruder is the part of a RepRap 3D Printer that uses heat to turn spools of plastic filament into objects.

 

Since the RepRap community embraces diversity and customization there are many different kinds of extruders available. Some of the most popular extruders are:

 

  • Wade's Geared Extruder
  • Greg's Accessible Extruder
  • Universal Extruder
  • Makerbot Extruder
  • Mini Extruder
