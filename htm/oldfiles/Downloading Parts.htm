Downloading parts

In order to print off the parts for your extruder, you will need to download the STL files from the Internet. You can get these files by searching for the extruder you want on a search engine, the RepRap wiki, the Thingverse website, or the Pirate Bay's "physibles" category.

 

Once you have downloaded your STL files, open them with your CAM tools convert STL files to g-code which can be interpreted by the RepRap machine. They include Skeinforge, RepSnapper, and RepRap Host Software.CAM program and follow its instructions.
