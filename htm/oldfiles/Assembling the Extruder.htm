Assembling the Extruder

1. Insert the short medium size screws into each of the holes at the base of the extruder.

2. Insert the long medium size screws into the four holes on the blocky part of the extruder so that they face outward like legs.

3. Use the small screws to lightly attach the stepper motor to the side of the extruder into the four small rectangle shaped holes. You will need to move this later, so don't screw on too tightly

4. Pick up the small gear and look for a tiny hole. Put the small hexagonal metal thing into the hole.

5. Slide the small gear onto the rod of the stepper motor.

6. Put the hobbed bolt through the hole in the large gear so that its hexagonal head fits securely inside the hexagonal groove in the gear.

7. Add three small washers to each of the long medium size screws.

8. Pick up the small one inch rod and place it inside a skateboard bearing.

9. Pick up the idler and slide in the rod and skateboard bearing.

10. Add another small washer and a wingnut to each screw on the outside of the idler.

11. Insert the large gear into the larger hole.

12. Line up the small gear and the large gear and then tighten the screws on the stepper motor.

13. Attach the extruder to the base on the machine.
